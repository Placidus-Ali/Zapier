# Project Title: 
Email Lead Collection

# Problem Statement: 
Leads are coming in from Google Form, but the sales team has to check Google Form response everytime to see new leads, causing delays and constant checking of Google Form.

# Objective: 
Sync every form submission to Email in real-time for easy team access.
