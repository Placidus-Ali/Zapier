# Project Title: 
Google Sheet Lead Collection

# Problem Statement: 
Leads are coming in from a website form, but the sales team has to log in to Zapier to see them, causing delays in follow-up.

# Objective: 
Sync every form submission to a Google Sheet in real-time for easy team access.
