# Project Title: 
Invoice & Attachment Management

# Problem Statement: 
Manually downloading invoice attachments from emails and uploading them to cloud storage is tedious and leads to disorganized files.

# Objective: 
Automatically capture all email attachments that contains "subject: Invoice" and upload them in a folder called Zapier in my Google Drive.
