# Project Title: 
Smart Newsletter Summarizer

# Problem Statement: 
You subscribe to 10+ newsletters but don't have time to read them all. Most of the content is fluff.

# Objective: 
Use AI to read incoming newsletters and send a 3-bullet point summary to Telegram.
